Community AI Strategy Framework

Designing Your AI Strategy

A six-step framework for community-focused organisations approaching AI thoughtfully, responsibly, and on their own terms.

01
Start here
Readiness & Impact Assessment
Scope potential use cases. Always ask if an analogue solution could work first. If AI is the right path, set a clear end goal and review dates.
Use case scoping Analogue-first Goal setting
02
Understand tradeoffs
AI Risk Assessment
Develop a thorough understanding of the risks, limitations, and tradeoffs for each use case. This informs every subsequent decision.
Risk mapping Limitations Tradeoffs
03
Choose your path
Buy vs. Build vs. Open Source
Building in-house keeps ownership close. Open source is often equally viable. Proprietary solutions should generally be the last resort.
In-house build Open source Procurement
04
Define the detail
Solution Design & Documentation
Finalise solution design with full documentation of data and model resources. Use data cards and model cards to ensure transparency.
Data cards Model cards Transparency
05
Build with oversight
Development & Deployment Lifecycle
Design a lifecycle with as much human observability as feasible — so behaviour can be monitored, questioned, and course-corrected throughout.
Human oversight Monitoring Course-correction
06
Plan the exit
Offboarding & Transition Assessment
Evaluate whether automation achieved its goals and plan the next step — ideally transitioning to a community-owned technology solution.
Impact review Exit planning Community ownership
